    Introduction

    Dr. Praveen Tiwari is a TV/ Print journalist for last 20 years. He’s an author of 6 books so far. During this period he has been associated with the organizations like IBN7, Live India, Zee Business, DD national, Sahara Samay etc. He’s the only anchor registered in world book and India book of records also for his PhD as the first ever PhD on political cartoons. As an academician he is associated with many institutes especially as the Director Mass communication with JJT University Rajasthan where many big names of media are pursuing their PhD under his guidance. As an author he has written many books and Satya ki Khoj and News Anchor: face of the news.. are notable work by him. He has anchored many popular shows. Awakening India is one show which is still telecasted on DD national. Innovate India with zee business was also very popular and Dr. Tiwari has started working on a book based on the same topic.
